Who We Are??

We are an MSME-registered Student-run Start-up with the aim of delivering high-tech robotics solutions to Real World problems.

Our Core Product is ScavengeX, a Semi-automatic robot that Converts Manholes into Machine holes and was inspired by the Indian government's Safai Mitra Suraksha Challenge. Mechanical Design, Materials Science, Electronics and Instrumentation, and Robotics Engineering are the 4 key domains wherein we work. We feel that Engineering has the Potential to Rehabilitate Sanitation Personnel.

ScavengeX is composed of two main units: a standing unit and a drone unit. The Drone Unit is assisted by the Standing Unit. KEY DIFFERENTIATORS

Semi Automatic

With its Standing and Drone Units, the Robot is Semi-Automatic, allowing Manual scavengers to be promoted to Machine Operators without losing their jobs.

User Friendly

The Bot consists of display and Control panel for easy user Interface.

Maximum Human Safety

The Bot has both Horizontal and Vertical Sewer line cleaning units, ensuring that there is no Manual intervention and Consequently Human Safety.

Cost Effective

Unlike existing options, this technology can also be used in Water-scarce areas and therefore can clean Horizontal sewer lines without the use of a Water jet. The Rover System was designed especially for Cleaning Horizontal Sewage System.
